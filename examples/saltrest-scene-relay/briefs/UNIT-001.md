# UNIT-001 — Beat 1: Dawn dread (relay, opens the scene)

## Objective
Open the scene. Establish gray dawn over Saltrest harbor through Mother Vell's eyes,
the wrong silence, the empty sea, and her dread of the water.

## Inputs / context
- CONTRACT.md (voice/POV/canon). SCENE.md is empty — you write the first beat.

## Approach (relay: APPEND to SCENE.md)
- Write ~180–260 words of continuous prose, third-person limited on Mother Vell,
  past tense, grim nautical folk-horror voice.
- Ground it in concrete sensory detail of the harbor at dawn and the unnatural
  stillness; convey Vell's fear of the water without explaining the plot.
- End the beat as a single fishing boat appears, rowing in against the pale light —
  hand off there for Beat 2.

## Constraints
- No beat heading, no stat blocks, no second person, no GM instructions.
- Use only canon names; introduce no new named characters. Do not yet show the catch.

- [ ] (assertional, voice) third-person limited on Mother Vell, past tense, grim nautical folk-horror; no second person, no game mechanics
- [ ] (assertional, content) establishes dawn harbor, unnatural stillness/wrongness, and Vell's dread of the water
- [ ] (assertional, handoff) ends with a single fishing boat appearing/rowing in against the light
- [ ] (assertional) only canon names; no new named characters; the bone-catch is NOT yet shown
